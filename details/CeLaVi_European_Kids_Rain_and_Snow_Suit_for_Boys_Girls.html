CeLaVi European Kids Rain and Snow Suit

Designed for both boys and girls, this rain suit delivers a perfect balance of style and practicality. With a detachable hood that features press buttons, it offers easy adjustments and a secure fit for growing little ones. The suit is waterproof with taped seams and a reinforced liner, allowing for seamless layering for warmth and comfort in colder weather.

Whether it's a rainy day, a snowy adventure, or a messy play session, this two-piece rain suit ensures your child stays safe and dry. The breathable fabric provides freedom of movement, while the elastic waistband and ankle cuffs ensure a snug and flexible fit. Reflective strips on the right arm and leg enhance visibility during outdoor play, making this a reliable choice for safety-conscious parents.

Perfect for everyday wear, school, and activities like camping, climbing, and hiking, this versatile rain gear adapts to your child's needs. The detachable hood and hook-and-loop wrist closure make it easy to customize for different occasions.
