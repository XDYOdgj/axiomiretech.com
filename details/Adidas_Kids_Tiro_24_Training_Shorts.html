Adidas Kids' Tiro 24 Training Shorts

Designed for young athletes, these shorts offer maximum comfort and performance. With cutting-edge technology and a focus on sustainability, they're perfect for everyday play and practice.

Peak Performance for Kids

The Tiro 24 shorts are engineered for active kids, combining a regular fit with an adjustable drawcord waist for a personalized, secure feel. Whether it's soccer, basketball, or track, these shorts keep your child moving freely without any restrictions.

Stay Dry, Stay in the Game

Featuring Air Readys technology, these shorts wick away sweat and keep your child cool and comfortable throughout play. The moisture-managing fabric is perfect for keeping them dry during intense activities, ensuring they stay focused and motivated.

Sustainable Style

Adidas has made a conscious choice by incorporating recycled materials into these shorts. This not only reduces environmental impact but also ensures your child has access to high-quality, eco-friendly athletic wear.

Perfect Fit, Every Time

The loose, balanced fit with an adjustable drawcord waist ensures flexibility and mobility for all ages. Reinforced pockets and a breathable design allow for effortless movement, making these shorts ideal for any kid's active lifestyle.

Designed with the needs of growing athletes in mind, the Adidas Kids' Tiro 24 Training Shorts are built to last and perform. From the moisture-managing fabric to the sustainable construction, every detail is crafted to support your child's athletic journey.
